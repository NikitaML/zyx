About ZYX
Foundation
Descriptor: ZYX Foundation – scale the future
ZYX Foundation is a non-profit organization intended to support ZYX environment and prospective technologies. ZYX Foundation do not control, manage or have a decisive influence on ZYX but is a part of institutional ZYX support system (consisted of a large amount of individuals, companies and others). Foundation registered in Switzerland in 2020. The ZYX Foundation is a Swiss foundation, supervised by the Swiss Federal Foundation SupervisoryAuthority. Our purpose is the promotion of the ZYX protocol through grants and other capital deployment vehicles. Our activity is not related to the receipt of profit or income from entrepreneurial activity. We accumulate voluntary donations from community members and then allocate these resources to projects and companies providing infrastructure development. So all conditions for long-term success of ZYX are ensured.
